About TigerGainz



Why Was TigerGainz Created?

Despite the COVID-19 pandemic and virtual classes, Vinay Konuru ’24 and Ben Cai ’24 still wanted to work out with friends. Unfortunately, everyone had different schedules, goals, and exercise preferences, especially in quarantine. As a result, Vinay and Ben had to exercise on their own, and they struggled to motivate themselves. Fortunately, Vinay and Ben met each other and other workout-eager classmates in the Class of 2024 Princeton group chat. After much planning, they finally found suitable workout partners.

But what if this process of finding workout buddies or groups with the most fitting workout partners was not only faster but more accessible?

That’s why Vinay, Ben, Eugene Liu ’24 and Elizabeth Medina ’24 co-founded TigerGainz in the hopes of creating a closer community on- and off-campus.

If you have any questions or concerns, please view our FAQ or visit the “Contact Us” section to email one of the co-founders or complete our feedback form.

Happy Exercising!

FAQs



Below are answers to FAQs. If you still have questions or concerns, please email one of the co-founders or complete our feedback form.

1. Who has access to my TigerGainz responses/workout preferences? What is my data used for?


All your TigerGainz responses/workout preferences are securely stored and solely used to create workout buddy matches and your TigerGainz profile. Your TigerGainz profile is only available to other TigerGainz users who have still not chosen a match.

2. My current Princeton workout buddy/group and I decided that we are not the best fit for each other. Can we request new Princeton workout buddies/groups? If so, how?


Yes, you both can request new Princeton workout buddies/groups. To do this, please go to the Database section and click the ““Take me off the database please” button. Then, click the sign up button to complete the form again (note: this will replace your old responses).

3. Can I edit my workout preferences? If so, how?


Yes, you can edit your workout preferences. To do this, please go to the Database section and click the “Take me off the database please” button. Then, click the sign up button to complete the form again (note: this will replace your old responses). However, this also means you may get different matches.

4. Why am I getting no matches on my initial search? What should I do? How long will I have to wait for a match?


In the unlikely scenario that TigerGainz finds no suitable matches upon form submission - most likely due to uncommon workout preferences - you should click the “Take me off the database please” button in the Database section and then complete form again with broader preferences. On the other hand, if you have already submitted the form and are still not getting matches, that means someone hasn’t chosen you to be their workout buddy or group yet. You may have to wait a few days. If you don’t want to wait for someone to choose you, please resubmit your form (see above) or check out the TigerGainz directory.

5. I haven’t heard back from TigerGainz in a while and I’m still looking for a match. What should I do?


Please go to the Database section and click the “Take me off the database please” button. Then, click the sign up button to complete the form again. Hopefully, if there are still Princetonians looking for a workout buddy or group, you should instantly get matches. Alternatively, you can check out the TigerGainz directory.

6. How can I maximize my chances of getting a match?


To maximize your chances of getting a match, you should select as many workout preferences you are comfortable with and choose the “average” or “most common” response.

7. Am I able to view other people’s profiles even if I haven’t matched with them?


Yes, you can view other people’s profiles in the directory even if they aren’t a match but only if you signed up for TigerGainz and you have not already found a match.
